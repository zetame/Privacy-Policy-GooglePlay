Developer Chaosbahun's privacy policy

Effective Date: 12/15/23 Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your personal information when you use our games or apps available on Google Play.
We do not collect any personal data from you, nor do we engage in any practices that require a detailed privacy policy. However, for transparency, we are providing this brief explanation.
Information We Do Not Collect
We do not collect any of the following information from you:
User accounts or logins
Gameplay data, such as progress, scores, or achievements
Device information, such as model, OS version, IP address, or location
Any other personal or identifiable information
Data Usage and Third-Party Sharing
We do not use any analytics tools, and we do not share any user data with third-party services or ad networks.
Security
While we don't collect any personal data, we take the security of your information seriously.
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect any potential data, even if it doesn't contain personal identifiers.
Changes to this Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on the Google Play store page for apps and games developed by Chaosbahun.
